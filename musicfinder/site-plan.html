Content

Home page

[This site is specifically for connecting students to teachers and will not endorse or favor any specific school or training method. In order to use this site, it will be important for users to either have their own studios or the students will need to have their own instruments. The site itself will not act as a marketplace for these kinds of transactions, only communications between teachers and students. ]
